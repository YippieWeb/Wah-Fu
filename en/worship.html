Worship

Worship services are held every Sunday morning at 10am and anyone is welcome to attend. Our meetings include prayer, singing (no musical instruments), Bible reading, sermons, Lord's Supper, and offerings. According to the Bible, we observe the Lord's Supper every day to remember the Lord.


Inclement Weather

Typhoon Signal No. 8 / Black Rainstorm  


1. Pay attention to the news from the Observatory, as well as the traffic and weather conditions on the road--safety first.

2. In case of the following situations, the meeting will be held online, and the Bible class and Sunday school will be cancelled.

    2.1 A black rain warning or typhoon signal No. 8 or above is hoisted at or before 7:00 am; or

    2.2 The Observatory is expected to hoist typhoon signal No. 8 or above before 10:00 am

3. If you have any questions, please contact us.


(Revised on 2020.06.21)


COVID-19

To protect the health of our participants, please note the following:

1. Please wear a mask.

2. Enter through the main entrance (Sunday School side), and check your body temperature before entering.

3. Sanitize your hands.

4. Follow the government's vaccine passport requirements.


Regarding the service protocols:

1. The person in charge will wear gloves while distributing the communion bread and chalice individually. The chalice provided is disposable, and the person in charge will collect it in a transparent plastic bag after everyone has partaken. Please place the used chalice in the bag yourself.

To ensure social distancing, a few seats will be temporarily removed.

To expedite the gathering, the Bible study will commence ten minutes after the meeting or may be canceled based on the circumstances.


If you are tested positive for COVID, please inform the chairman.


Thanks for your cooperation.

Translation corrections:

242 主啊! 救我!

副歌本作「主是管理海陸的神萬」，詞與曲不協。英文原詞是 ‘The Master of ocean and earth and skies’，現改為「主是管理海陸空的神」。


262 我真實愛耶穌

歌詞有三節，有副歌有兩節，不配搭。根據英文原詞，副歌只有一節，現刪去第二節，保留第一節。


314 這世界非我家

第二節本作「我將前住歡聚」，殊不可解，現改為「我將前往歡聚」。
