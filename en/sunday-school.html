Sunday School

Sunday school is divided into high, middle and elementary classes, conducted in groups, starting at 10:30 every Sunday morning. The purpose is to let children and teenagers begin to know the Lord Jesus Christ, study the Bible, and prepare to become Christians when they grow up. We welcome you to study the Bible and the truth together!
