Course Introduction

Bible Correspondence Course


  • Do you want to study the word of God (the Bible) systematically?
  • Do you not quite understand the truth of God and need some learning tools to understand the truth of the Bible?

This course is designed for you!

Course Set-up

The aim of this course is to assist individuals seeking knowledge about the Bible in comprehending it and gaining a systematic understanding of the Church of Christ. Regardless of your geographical location, you can actively engage in our course by accessing it online, free from the constraints of time or place.

Our Goal

Our utmost aspiration is that individuals who have yet to embrace Jesus Christ will join the church and become followers of Christianity upon completing this course and gaining a comprehensive understanding of the Bible. Furthermore, if you are already a Christian, this course will empower you to deepen your comprehension of the teachings of Jesus Christ and the principles upheld by the Church.

Why This Course

This course is entirely free, with no additional procedures required! It consists of a total of 27 lessons. Once you complete a lesson, simply send your answers to Wah Fu Church of Christ via email or post. Our dedicated church members will promptly review your answers and address any questions you have regarding the course. Don't pass up this invaluable opportunity to delve into the study of the Bible!

Reward

Upon completion of the 27 lessons, you will receive a splendid Bible containing both the New and Old Testaments as a reward. However, the true value lies not solely in the physical gift, but rather in the transformation of your life in service to God, leading to the attainment of His blessings and salvation.
